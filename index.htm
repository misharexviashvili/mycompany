Some words about us

We are company providing digital services, including programming, sound design and digital art. MyCompany LLC was founded in September 2022, by Mikhaeli Rekhviashvili, our current CEO and owner. Our main goal is to provide high quality in short time. We work on small, medium and even large projects. We have completed projects for many international companies, including entities from EU and USA .
